What It Is

A Complete Brand Document Pipeline

Documents Forger is a self-hosted tool that automates the process of creating brand-consistent documents. Instead of manually extracting colors and fonts from brand guidelines, the AI does it in seconds.

Feed it your content — Markdown, Word, PDF, or PowerPoint — and it renders it into a branded PDF or Word document using the extracted design system. No design skills required.

1

Extract

AI analyzes a website or PDF and pulls out every design token — colors, typography, spacing, shadows, border radii.

2

Review & Refine

Preview the extracted design system. Chat with AI to adjust colors, swap fonts, or tweak any detail before proceeding.

3

Generate Templates

Approve the design system and AI generates branded report (A4) and slides (16:9) HTML templates automatically.

4

Render Documents

Upload any content. It gets injected into your brand template and rendered as a PDF or Word document you can download.

Features

Everything You Need

From brand extraction to document delivery, every step is handled.

AI Design Extraction

Powered by Google Gemini. Provide a URL or upload a PDF, and the AI extracts a complete design token set — primary, secondary, and accent colors with exact hex values, font families with CDN sources, type scale, spacing, shadows, and border radii.

Interactive AI Review

After extraction, review the design system visually. Not satisfied? Open a conversation with the AI — describe what you want changed in plain English. The AI updates the design system in up to 5 refinement rounds.

Multi-Format Input

Upload content as Markdown, plain text, Word (.docx), PDF, or PowerPoint (.pptx). The system converts everything to a common format before rendering, so you can work with whatever you already have.

Report & Slides Templates

AI generates two template types per brand: A4 portrait reports and 16:9 landscape slides. Both use the extracted design system with proper typography hierarchy, consistent spacing, and branded color application.

Inline Editing

View generated documents in the browser. Click any text element to edit it directly — headings, paragraphs, list items, table cells. Changes auto-save after 1.5 seconds and you can re-render the PDF at any time.

PDF & Word Download

Download finished documents as PDF (rendered via headless Chrome for pixel-perfect output) or Word (.docx) format with preserved styles, fonts, and colors. Both formats are generated server-side.

How to Use It

Four Steps to Branded Documents

The entire process takes under 5 minutes from start to finished document.

1

Create a Brand

Enter a brand name, provide one or more website URLs, or upload PDF brand guidelines. You can use any combination of sources.

2

Review the Design System

The AI extracts colors, fonts, and spacing into a visual reference sheet. Review it, or chat with the AI to make adjustments before approving.

3

Generate a Document

Choose report or slides format, paste your content or upload a file. The system applies the brand template and renders it automatically.

4

Edit & Download

View the result in the browser, make inline edits if needed, then download as PDF or Word. Re-render anytime after editing.
